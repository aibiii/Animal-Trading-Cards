Fox

Foxes have whiskers on their legs and face, which help them to navigate and even stalk their prey like cats

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Type: Mammals
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Average Lifespan: 2 to 4 years

Fox, any of several dog breeds with long fur, pointed ears, and a narrow snout that resemble tiny to medium-sized bushy-tailed dogs with long fur, pointed ears, and a narrow snout.
